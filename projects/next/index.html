NEXT Interior Design

Description

A fictitious architecture firm, NEXT, has decided to relocate to the 32nd floor of USC Tower in downtown Los Angeles. The 11,300 SQF space will accommodate 35-45 employees. Its design must embrace collaborative and flexible ways of working in order to facilitate the innovation of tomorrow.

Students, through this competition, were tasked with researching trends and challenges facing the creative workplace. They would create the ideal office concept and design through evidence-based design and the programming information. Finally, a presentation package explaining the process and containing construction documents and renderings would be compiled for delivery to the client.

Design Concept

With design, there is the initial messiness as ideas ricochet off the environment. Concepts are both mixed and discarded, creating something edible but not quite perfect. When all possibilities are explored, the concept is placed in the oven, where it solidifies under intense heat.

The end product is golden, beautiful and delicious.
