Architectural Models

Description

I designed these models for my high school architecture studio.
The ecohouse is designed for a specific site on Yerba Buena Island adjacent to San Francisco, with expansive views of the Bay and of the Golden Gate Bridge. The house makes use of sustainable features such as passive ventilation and directional tree cover. The floor to ceiling windows on the first floor help connect the interior with the exterior, and the second story bedrooms directly face the most picturesque views.
The treehouse design aims to maximize floor space and natural light in the single room of the treehouse. The angle of the front sloping wall is carried through the structural members to maintain visual consistency.
