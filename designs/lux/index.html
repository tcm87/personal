Lux Lounge Design

A lounge concept for a modern student housing complex in Ithaca, New York. The design facilitates student activities in novel and elegant ways.
Duration

January 2018 / 3 Weeks

Role / Skills

Project with Reishan McIntosh | Interior Design, CAD, Rendering

Tools

Rhino, V-Ray, Photoshop

Introduction

The lounge design was part of an interior design competition for a high-end student apartment complex in Ithaca, NY. Reishan McIntosh and I collaborated on the project.

Our design aims to accomodate for the range of student activities in a multipurpose lounge. The design details

Research

To frame our reference, we visited student housing-- dorms, apartments, and Greek Life houses to see how the common areas are used. We talked with users of these spaces and observed the space in use to gain a proper understanding.

Ideation

Design Features

The wooden ceiling slats visually divide the lounge, following the room contours. As you walk through the lounge, the ceiling changes dimension and visual weight. Each view offers a new perspective.
