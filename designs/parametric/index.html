DESIGN ABOUT

An alternate design langauge.

Working with Professor So-Yeon Yoon and Ph.D student Yixiao Wang, I have explored the use of parametric modeling for interior design and installations. The three of us are co-authoring a paper for the Design Communication Association (DCA) conference 2018 detailing a pedagogical method to implement parametric modeling that differs from the typical design langauge of concept sketching and hand modeling.

Room to Grow

Having learned the basics of Grasshopper, I will continue to develop my understanding and capabilities with the software. I have some promising ideas I want to bring to fruition that benefit from the characteristics of parametric design. Stay tuned as I update this page with my explorations.
