A fictitious architecture firm, NEXT, has decided to relocate to the 32nd floor of USC Tower in downtown Los Angeles. The 11,300 SQF space will accommodate 35-45 employees. Its design must embrace collaborative and flexible ways of working in order to facilitate the innovation of tomorrow.

As the project for DEA 3301 UX Design Studio, I was tasked with researching trends and challenges facing the creative workplace. I then created the ideal office concept and design through evidence-based design and the programming information. Finally, I created a presentation package explaining the process and containing construction documents and renderings for the fictitious client.
