Design Lead for Religio

Religio's mission is to boost engagement between churches and its members. Through a fully developed mobile and web platform, Religio increases engagement between churches and its members, connects people to their religious circles and brings innovation to charity. I've been working as the lead designer to design a mobile app, web platform, CMS software, and general branding.

Below is a sample of my design work. For more information, contact me, as some my designs have yet to be implemented.

Web Platform

Aimed at building community within your religious organization. Group messaging, giving tools, and networking functionality allow church members to find mentors within their own church to help them grow spirituality and professionally. Additionally, Religio helps connect people with similar interests and increase volunteering opportunities. Parishioners can grow deeper in their faith through discussion, videos, news, radio and much more.Â 

Mobile App

Religio's core product. Currently available on Android and iPhone, the mobile app serves as one place for all your church needs. Find news, radio, and tweets related to Catholicism, get access to all your localized religion and church media, schedule and find events at your church, and give and participate in fundraisers.
