BLOG DESIGN INFO

A passion project, explored.

Intunity was first created by three student developers at Cornell University’s 3 Day Startup. The original idea was for an individualized music stream curated specifically by the user’s friends. The users could only post once a day in order to maintain the importance of each song. I was brought on the team to do the visual design for Intunity. What began as an attempt to create a visually intriguing website evolved into a rehashing of the entire concept.
