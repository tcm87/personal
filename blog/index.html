Custom Camera Case, Pt. 1

June 2017

Before I had even bought my first camera, I thought about creating a custom made camera case. This case for my non-existent camera would fit to the milimeter, be easily wieldable and comfortable so I could explore a new city, or throw it on my back during a bike ride.

I’ve worked with wood, steel, titanium even. I’m very familiar with the processes involved to shape and combine these materials. However, I’ve never worked with fiberglass before. I thought fiberglass would be the perfect candidate for my camera case- durable, moldable, and not too expensive. Here is the first part of the fiberglass camera case.
