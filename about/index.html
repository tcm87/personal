EXPERIMENTS ABOUT

bringing concepts to fruition through thoughtful design and a clarity of process

I am currently a technical designer at Stand and Build, where I work with blue chip fashion houses to bring their seasonal and window displays to life.

I graduated from Cornell University, B.S. in Design + Environmental Analysis, minor in Architecture. My past professional experience includes spatial/installation design work with Dutch brand agency Edhv, product design with Universal Sonar Mount, and user experience design at Salesforce.

I am most passionate about projects that take a concept and completely, thoroughly, and methodically carry it through to a final design.

I can be reached at tcm87@cornell.edu
