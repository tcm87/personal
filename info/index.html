BLOG DESIGN INFO

It all comes back to storytelling.

For me, a success in my designs or my writing, or videos, is when I am able to capture some emotion and share it. That is good storytelling, and good design.

Additionally, I care about innovation-- my designs should be more than beautiful, or functional, but they should bring something new to this world. I care about taking a concept and completely, thoroughly, and methodically carrying it through to a final design. Of course, there will be transgressions. But, if in the end, my final design is a reflection of the process, I have succeeded.
